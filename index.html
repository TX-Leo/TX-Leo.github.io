Energy-Based Imitation Learning

Minghuan Liu, Tairan He, Minkai Xu, Weinan Zhang

ArXiv Preprint

We proposed EBIL, a two-step solution for imitation learning: first estimate the energy of expert’s occupancy measure, and then take the energy to construct a surrogate reward function as a guidance for the agent to learn the desired policy. We achieve comparable performance against SOTA methods.
